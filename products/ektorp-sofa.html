Assembly Instructions

1

Unpack Components

Carefully remove all packaging. Check that you have: frame, legs (8), cushions, and hardware packet.

2

Attach Legs

Screw the 8 legs into the pre-drilled holes on the bottom of the frame. Hand-tighten first, then use provided wrench for final tightening.

3

Position Cushions

Place seat cushions on the frame, then add back cushions. Ensure covers are properly zipped.

4

Final Check

Ensure all legs are secure and sofa is stable. Adjust cushions for even appearance.

Assembly Time: Approximately 15-20 minutes | Tools Required: Included in package

Care & Maintenance

Cover Care:

  • Remove covers and machine wash at 104°F (40°C)
  • Use mild detergent, no bleach
  • Tumble dry low heat or air dry
  • Iron on medium heat if needed

Frame Care:

  • Vacuum regularly to remove dust
  • Wipe frame with damp cloth as needed
  • Rotate cushions periodically for even wear

Maintenance Tips:

  • Tighten leg screws every 6 months
  • Fluff cushions weekly
  • Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent fading
  • Use furniture protectors under legs on hardwood floors
