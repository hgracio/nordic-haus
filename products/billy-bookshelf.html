Assembly Instructions

1

Prepare Components

Unpack all parts carefully. You should have: 2 side panels, 1 top, 1 bottom, 1 back panel, 5 shelves, shelf pins, and wall mounting hardware.

2

Build Frame

Connect side panels to top and bottom using the provided cam-lock fasteners. Tighten firmly but don't overtighten.

3

Install Back Panel

Attach the back panel using small nails. Ensure it's square by measuring diagonals - they should be equal.

4

Add Shelves & Mount

Insert shelf pins at desired heights and place shelves. Stand unit upright and secure to wall using included brackets. This step is mandatory for safety.

Assembly Time: 30-45 minutes | Tools Required: Included + drill for wall mounting

Care & Maintenance

Cleaning:

  • Wipe with a damp cloth and mild detergent
  • Dry immediately with clean cloth
  • Do not use abrasive cleaners
  • Avoid excessive moisture

Maintenance:

  • Check wall mounting every 6 months
  • Distribute weight evenly across shelves
  • Don't exceed 30 lbs per shelf
  • Adjust shelf height by moving pins

Safety Tips:

  • Always mount to wall - tipping hazard!
  • Keep heavy items on lower shelves
  • Don't allow children to climb on unit
  • Replace wall anchors if moving to new location
